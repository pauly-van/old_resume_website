Parrot

Chrome browser extension

Have you ever found yourself trying to pair your television, setup box, chromecast, amazon fire, or gaming console to an existing streaming account? Does these streaming accounts often ask you to verify a code on your tv screen to cast a video? Pair it, with Parrot! The google chrome extension that repeats your code to its desired account. It covers a wide range of services like YouTube, Netflix, Amazon Video, Hulu, Roku, and more!
